Description

These high-quality medical nitrile gloves are made in Thailand, offering strength, durability and barrier protection that prevents skin contact to ensure contamination does not occur. They have been designed for maximum sensitivity, dexterity and comfort, and offer increased elasticity and flexibility to reduce the risk of fatigue when in use for extended periods of time.
